How to Buy MEGATRON on the Tron Network

Follow these steps to buy MEGATRON, the meme token of resistance, on the Tron network. This guide will take you through everything from setting up your wallet to buying MEGATRON on Sun.io.

Step 1: Set Up a Tron-Compatible Wallet

Before you can buy MEGATRON, you'll need a wallet that supports the Tron network. We recommend using TronLink for the best experience.

  • Download TronLink from the TronLink website.
  • Install the browser extension or mobile app, and create your wallet.
  • Securely back up your recovery phrase or private key!

Step 2: Get TRX (Tron) for MEGATRON

You'll need TRX to buy MEGATRON and cover transaction fees on the Tron network. You can get TRX in two ways: Buy directly or Bridge other crypto into TRX.

Option 1: Buy TRX on a Centralized Exchange

  • Sign up on a CEX like Binance or KuCoin.
  • Deposit fiat or crypto and buy TRX.
  • Withdraw TRX to your Tron wallet address (from TronLink).

Option 2: Bridge Crypto to TRX Using HoudiniSwap

  • Visit HoudiniSwap and connect your wallet.
  • Select the crypto you have (BTC, ETH, USDT) and convert it to TRX.
  • Confirm the swap and wait for TRX to arrive in your Tron wallet.

Step 3: Access SunSwap on Sun.io

Once you've got TRX in your wallet, you're ready to swap it for MEGATRON. Head to Sun.io and follow these steps:

  • Go to the SunSwap page and connect your TronLink wallet.
  • Select TRX as the currency you want to swap.
  • Paste the contract address for MEGATRON into the “To” field.
  • Enter the amount of TRX you want to swap and confirm the transaction.

Step 4: Verify Your MEGATRON Balance

After the swap is complete, you’ll want to check if the MEGATRON tokens have arrived in your wallet. If you don’t see them, you may need to manually add the token using the contract address.
